# Integração com o Adobe Campaign {#integrating-campaign}

O AEM as a Cloud Service não oferece suporte à integração com o [Adobe Campaign Classic](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/docs/experience-manager-65/administering/integration/campaignonpremise.html) ou [Adobe Campaign Standard.](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/docs/experience-manager-65/administering/integration/campaignstandard.html)
